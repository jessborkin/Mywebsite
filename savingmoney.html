FIND CHEAP FLIGHTS

Nowerdays we have no shortage of low cost airlines, ocassionally you will find crazy promotions with 5GBP seats, my advice is to grab these even if its month in advance because once there gone there gone. Its know when you see a cheap flight adding luggage can sometimes be 10x the price of teh flight itself. my advice is to travel light - if need be take only hand luggage.

Build up points, if you fly regularly always signup to the airlines group flying club. when you build up enought points you can exchange them for free flights or upgrades
