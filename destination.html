Where Will You Travel Too

The world is one big open adventure, there is almost no corner of the world you can't travel to, from one hour flights to scotlland to 24 hour flights to Australia.. you can even go to the Antartica but that would include some sea travel. What's imporant to check before you decide on a destination is to research, see if you need a visa, check how often flights are, you may even need vaccines to travel to certain areas. Know before hand the currency and even laws and restrictions of the country you planning to travel to as it may religious or a holy place, so always be respectful.
